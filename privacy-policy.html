This Privacy Policy describes how Starlit Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use any of our mobile applications (collectively, the "Apps"), including but not limited to Nut & Bolt: Color Puzzle.

2. Information We Collect

Our Apps are designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not:

Our Apps may automatically collect certain non-personal information to improve your experience:

Our Apps integrate third-party services that may collect information independently. These services include:

📢 Google AdMob — We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and ad interaction data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

🛡️ Firebase Crashlytics — We may use Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to improve App stability. This data does not include personal information.

The information collected is used to:

4. Advertising

Our Apps are free-to-play and supported by advertisements. We use Google AdMob to serve ads, which may include:

  • Rewarded Video Ads: Users voluntarily watch ads to earn in-game rewards

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
